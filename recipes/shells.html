Stuffed Shells

Easy, cheesy comfort food

This recipe is super simple to make and yields plenty of servings that taste even better as leftovers!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag pasta shells
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • 500g ricotta
  • 250g shredded mozzarella
  • 200g parmesan
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh parlsey (as needed)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta shells until just before al dente, set aside. Preheat overn to 350 degrees.
  2. mix ricotta, 3/4 of the mozzarella, parmesan and egg in a bowl. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Put 1/3 of the marinara sauce at the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish. Stuff 2tbsp of the cheese mixture in each shell and add to the dish.
  4. Once all shells have been added, cover them with the remaining marinara and mozzarella and bake for 35 min.
  5. Let cool for 5 minutes, sprinkle with parsley and serve!
