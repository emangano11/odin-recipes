Guacamole

A fresh favorite

Everyone loves guacamole, a fresh addition to any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 small red onion
  • 1 jalapeño
  • 2 limes
  • 1 bunch cilantro

Steps

  1. Mash 2 avocados in a large bowl until smooth.
  2. Chop red onion and seeded jalapeño and add to mashed avocados. Mix well.
  3. Add cilantro and juice from 2 limes. Season generously with salt.
  4. Serve with additional cilantro on top.
