Lentil Soup

A healthy and delicious meal

This lentil soup recipe uses only a few ingredients but is a deliciously satisfying vegetarian meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of green lentils
  • 3 carrots
  • 3 stalks of celery
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes

Steps

  1. Chop carrots, celery, and yellow onion. Sautee in 3 tbsp olive oil for 15 minutes or until soft.
  2. Add stock, can of chopped tomatoes, salt and pepper and bring to a boil.
  3. Once boiling, rinse lentils and add to pot. Cover and simmer for 35 minutes or until soft.
  4. Serve with parsley, parmesan cheese, or a squeeze of lemon juice.
